Dogs are known as man’s best friend. Dogs are loyal and adorable. Sometimes we treat our dogs not just as pets, but as a part of our family. They are man’s best friend, but can they also be man’s best neighbor? In the Philippines the most common dogs are called “Aspin”. There is an estimated value of 11.6 million stray dogs in this country. Most of the stray dogs are Aspin. Apparently, these dogs caused a lot of trouble and problems in the society and community.

We chose Caniogan, Calumpit, Bulacan because the problem is very imminent there. We will put pictures and other types of media in the website to expose the problems that stray dogs have caused like pooping in public, eating trashes, and spreading trash everywhere. We are doing this to promote saving the welfare of stray dogs. 

Dogs can be easy to domesticate, they have a trait that let them to be tamed. But stray dogs are very common in our community that most of stray dogs are homeless. Our team is proposing a plan that will save the dog welfare. Saving the dog welfare is one of the problems that we must address. We need to be aware that it is their nature to do those things. Every Barangay/community must have a place for these do. Some families need to adopt dogs to lessen the population of dogs that are roaming the in the community.
Stray dogs must be saved. They do not have their home and they have a high chance to die because of starvation or illness. They are not trained to do things in our social norms. We must act to save the dogs in need and only us humans have a heart to save those stray dogs in our community.
